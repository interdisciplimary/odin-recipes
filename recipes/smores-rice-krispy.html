S'mores Rice Krispy

Description

Rice Krispies are easy to make. And making them in the style of S'mores isn't too hard either! Let's get to it.

Ingredients

  • Butter, 4 tablespoons
  • Marshmallows, 4 cups
  • Marshmallow Bits, 3/4 cups
  • Vanilla Extract, 1 teaspoon
  • Rice Krispies, 3 cups
  • Golden Grahams, 2 cups
  • Chocolate Chips, 3/4 cups

Steps

  1. Mix together the rice krispies, Golden Grahams, Marshmallow Bits, and chocolate chips. Set aside.
  2. Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Turn the heat down to the lowest setting and add the marshmallows.
  4. Stir until almost totally melted, then add the vanilla extract. Continue stirring until the marshmallows are all melted, slow and steady.
  5. Once fully melted, take them off heat and pour over the rice krispy mixture. Stir lightly to combine.
  6. Dump into a lightly greased 9x9 pan and let sit for 5 minutes. Then go and lightly press the mixture into the pan with greased hands.
  7. Allow then to cool and set about 20 minutes.
  8. Enjoy!
