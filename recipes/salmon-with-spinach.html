Salmon with Spinach

Description

Salmon is my favorite fish. And I happen to like spinach. I do not like when recipe websites get into prose about things unrelated to the recipe. So without further ado...

Ingredients

  • Oil, 1 teaspoon
  • Salmon, two 4-oz fillets
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter, 1 tablespoon
  • Lemon juice, 1 teaspoon
  • Spinach

Prep

  1. Thaw the salmon in the refrigerator 24 hours in advance

Steps for Cooking the Salmon

  1. Bring the salmon to room temperature 10 minutes before cooking
  2. Warm a large nonstick skillet with oil over medium-low heat
  3. Pat the fish on both sides with paper towels to get rid of excess water
  4. Season the fish on both sides with salt and pepper
  5. Raise the heat to medium-high and place the salmon skin-side up in the pan
  6. Cook until golden brown on 1 side, about 4 minutes
  7. With 1 minute remaining on the first side, place butter in the pan
  8. Turn the fish over and place lemon juice in pan
  9. Cook until desired crispness, about 3 minutes

Steps for Cooking the Spinach

  1. Bring the temperature down to low
  2. Place spinach in the same skillet
  3. Saute until desired texture
